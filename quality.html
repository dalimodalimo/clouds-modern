Quality & Safety

Clouds Modern SPC is committed to the highest standards in workplace safety and quality assurance throughout all phases of our operations.

Compliance & Standards

  • Adherence to the Environment, Health, and Safety (EHS) Management System
  • Alignment with national and international quality benchmarks
  • Strict enforcement of company safety and quality procedures

Risk Identification & Hazard Control

  • Continuous monitoring of sites to identify and mitigate risks
  • Detailed incident and accident investigations
  • Implementation of Corrective and Preventive Actions (CAPA)

Safety Training & Awareness

  • Daily safe start meetings with all contractors
  • Regular site safety presentations and awareness sessions
  • Ongoing education to enhance contractor compliance and safety culture

Workplace Responsibility

  • Enforcing road safety and workplace health rules
  • Promoting personal responsibility for health and safety
  • Maintaining a secure and productive work environment
